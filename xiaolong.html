Dr. Xiaolong Sun



Biography: I was born in Qingdao, China. I worked for Professor Xuhong Qian as a Master's student in East China University of Science and Technology and then was accepted as a PhD student working for Professor Tony D. James in 2012, University of Bath, United Kingdom. In 2015, I started my work as a joint Postdoc for Professor Eric V. Anslyn and Andy Ellington.


Contact: xxs2759@utexas.edu


Degrees: MSc in Applied Chemistry, East China University of Science and Technology; PhD in Organic chemistry, University of Bath.
